### Aetheria User Guide

Welcome to Aetheria, Explorer! This guide will help you get started on your journey to discover the world in a completely new way.

#### **1. Your Explorer Profile**

When you first join Aetheria, you are an **Explorer**. Your profile is your home base—it's where you can manage your settings, view your created itineraries, and track your progress through the Aetheria ecosystem.

#### **2. Core Features**

As an Explorer, you have access to a rich suite of AI-powered tools designed to enhance your travels:

*   **AI Itinerary:** The heart of Aetheria. Simply provide a destination, duration, and a "vibe" (e.g., "Relaxing," "Adventurous," "Historical"), and let our AI craft a personalized journey for you.
*   **Explorer Dashboard:** Your main hub, where you'll find suggestions, see active bounties, and access all of Aetheria's features.
*   **Landmark Lens:** See a landmark? Take a picture, and our AI will tell you its history, architecture, and fun facts.
*   **Heritage Mirror:** Upload a photo of yourself in a destination and see yourself reimagined in historical attire from that region.
*   **Vibe Market:** Browse and purchase unique "vibes"—curated aesthetic and experiential packages that can be applied to your itineraries and influence AI suggestions.
*   **AR Wayfinding:** Get immersive, augmented reality directions to your next destination, making navigation an adventure in itself.

#### **3. The Aetheria Community**

Aetheria is more than just an app; it's a global community of travelers.

*   **The Bounty Board:** Complete challenges in various locations to earn experience points (XP) and unique rewards. Bounties might range from "Take a photo of the sunrise over the Eiffel Tower" to "Try three different kinds of street food in Tokyo."
*   **Local Heroes:** Connect with verified local experts for authentic experiences. These are trusted community members who can offer everything from a home-cooked meal to a guided tour of hidden gems.
*   **Traveler's Guilds:** Join groups of like-minded explorers. Whether you're a foodie, a history buff, or an adrenaline junkie, there's a guild for you to share tips and stories.

#### **4. Staying Safe**

Your safety is paramount. The **Scam Alert** system, powered by admin verification and community reports, will notify you of known scams in your current location, helping you travel with more confidence.

#### **5. User Roles**

*   **Explorer:** This is the default role for all users.
*   **Partner:** A verified account for organizations like tourism boards or businesses.
*   **Admin:** A platform administrator with moderation and management privileges.

Active and trusted members of the community may be promoted to roles like "Local Hero" or even "Admin" based on their contributions. Keep exploring, sharing, and helping fellow travelers!
## Navigating the Ecosystem
Aetheria Explorer has successfully scaled to over 300 distinctive features. To streamline your journey, the main sidebar menu dynamically categorizes tools into 9 main hubs: Core Hub, AR & Immersive, Gastronomy, Wellness & Fitness, Nightlife & Entertainment, Local Culture, Transit & Finance, Safety & Legal, and Explorer Utilities.

## Aetheria+ and Premium Booking Passes
Advanced platform capabilities, including generative AI planners, AR Time-Lapses, and Landmark Lenses, route through Aetheria+.
- **Global Subscription**: A universal subscription unlocks everything worldwide.
- **Booking Passes**: If you lack a global subscription, you can purchase an isolated Premium Pass mapped strictly to a specific itinerary booking ID.

## Integrated Travel Insurance
Navigate to the Travel Insurance hub to synthesize a unified policy over all your individual trip bookings cleanly. Review basic, premium, and extreme adventure coverage tiers, and seamlessly checkout natively inside the app using the new, highly secure Aetheria Payments form.

# Backpacker: Comprehensive Feature List

## 1. Odyssey Orchestration
- **Visual Path Finder**: Synthesize journeys from social media inspiration photos ($5 fee).
- **Vibe Marketplace**: Peer-to-peer trading of high-fidelity itinerary templates ($2.99).
- **AI Odyssey Planner**: Psychologically-tailored daily schedules.
- **Smart Wallet**: Multi-currency hub with Scan & Pay and instant exchanges.
- **Budget Synthesis**: AI Financial Oracle providing "Smart Swaps."

## 2. The Intelligence Lens
- **Chronos Lens**: AR History Portal for landmark reconstruction.
- **Vision AI Hub**: Contextual analysis for Menus, Art, and Souvenirs.
- **Pharmacy Scout**: Multimodal medication decoding with regional mapping.
- **Language Lab**: Survival kits with native TTS pronunciation.
- **Culture Scout**: Local etiquette and social DNA guide.

## 3. Creative Synthesis
- **AI Trip Odyssey**: 8-second cinematic teasers (Horizontal & 9:16 Vertical).
- **Postcard Studio**: Transform snaps into high-art postcards (Imagen 4).
- **Heritage Mirror**: Historical selfie transformation into traditional dress.
- **Explorer XP**: Gamified "Season Pass" progression system.

## 4. Immersive & Social
- **AR Wayfinding**: Street-level data overlays.
- **Vibe Radar**: Real-time social matching with fellow explorers.
- **Local Legends AI**: GPS-triggered folklore narrations.
- **Destination Events**: Collaborative planning with AI Expense Splitting.

## 5. Explorer's Utility
- **SOS Panic Hub**: Multi-tier emergency response (Silent & Audible).
- **Visa Architect**: Real-time cross-border compliance and document check.
- **Logistics Radar**: Live air, rail, and sea status board.
- **Carbon Synthesis**: Impact calculation and one-tap wallet offsetting.
- **Digital Tailor**: Zero-luggage wardrobe rentals delivered to destination.

1.  **Observe**: Scroll through the features to see what the AI can do for your specific travel style.

### 1.2 Auth Hub (`/login`, `/signup`)

**Key Features**:

## 2. Command & Control (The Central Hub)

### 2.1 Explorer Dashboard (`/dashboard`)
**The Purpose**: Your central nervous system. Everything you are doing, have done, or are about to do is visible here.
**Visual Representation**: A multi-column layout featuring your "Season Pass" progression and a real-time "Vibe Radar."
**Key Features**:
*   **Season Pass (XP)**: A gamified progress bar. Every scan, booking, and synthesis earns you "Explorer XP." Leveling up unlocks higher-fidelity tools.
*   **Vibe Radar**: A social discovery node that shows fellow explorers nearby who share your itinerary's "psychological vibe."
*   **Partner Spotlights**: Context-aware deals (e.g., if you're in Paris, you see a spotlight for a hidden chateau).
**How to Use**:
1.  **Monitor**: Check your current level and active trip pass.
2.  **Launch**: Use the feature groups (Plan, Explore, Creative, Tools) to navigate to specific modules.

### 2.2 AI Odyssey Planner (`/itinerary-generator`)
**The Purpose**: To synthesize a daily schedule that matches your current psychological state.
**Visual Representation**: A split-screen interface with a configuration form on the left and a live-rendering itinerary on the right.
**Key Features**:
*   **Psychological Vibe Input**: Instead of just "Adventure," you can enter "I'm feeling anxious and need calm." The AI adjusts the pace of the trip accordingly.
*   **Dynamic Refinement**: A chat box below the result where you can say "Add more street food" or "Make it cheaper," and the AI re-synthesizes the logic in real-time.
**How to Use**:
1.  **Configure**: Enter destination, dates, and budget.
2.  **Synthesize**: Hit the button and wait for the AI to build the day-by-day plans.
3.  **Adjust**: Use the "Refine" box to tweak the output.

## 3. The Intelligence Lens (Vision & Culture)

### 3.1 Vision AI Hub / Scanner (`/scanner`)
**The Purpose**: To interpret the physical world. Your camera becomes an interface for deep cultural data.
**Visual Representation**: A high-tech "Radar" interface with a scanning line that sweeps across the camera feed.
**Key Features**:
*   **Menu Explorer**: Translates and explains the ingredients and "foodie context" of foreign dishes.
*   **Street Art Decoder**: Identifies the social and political narrative behind urban murals.
*   **Chronos Lens**: Identifies a landmark and renders a "Historical Reconstitution"—showing you the 100-year-old version of the spot you're standing in.
*   **Pharmacy Scout**: Decodes foreign medication labels into your native language (Informational only).
**How to Use**:
1.  **Select Lens**: Choose your mode (Menu, Art, Meds, etc.).
2.  **Capture**: Align the subject and hit "Analyze."
3.  **Read**: Review the "Intelligence Node" that appears with the result.

### 3.2 Intel Hub / Local Guide (`/guide`)
**The Purpose**: Mastering the market psychology and social DNA of a city.
**Visual Representation**: A tabbed interface allowing you to switch between a chatbot, a guide, and a coach.
**Key Features**:
*   **Haggling Coach**: Enter an item and its price. The AI provides a "Target Price" and specific negotiation tactics in the local language.
*   **Culture Scout**: A deep-dive into local "Do's and Don'ts" to avoid social faux pas.
*   **Local Insider**: Chat with an AI persona that represents the "soul" of the city.
**How to Use**:
1.  **Inquiry**: Ask the Insider for "Hidden Gems" that aren't in guidebooks.
2.  **Coach**: Before buying a souvenir, use the Haggling Coach to get your "Walk Away" price.

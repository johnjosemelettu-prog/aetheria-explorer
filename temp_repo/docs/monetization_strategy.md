# Backpacker: Monetization & Financial Projections 2026

## 1. Revenue Architecture

We replace static monthly subscriptions with **Active Journey Passes** and transactional synthesis fees.

### 1.1 The "Trip Pass" Model (B2C)
*   **Discovery Pass ($19/trip):** Unlocks AR/VR, Folklore, Packing Agent, and Layover Odyssey. 
    *   *Target Conversion:* 30% of active users.
*   **Elite Pass ($49/trip):** Unlocks Veo 3 Video, Heritage Mirror, Zero-fee bookings, and Priority Concierge.
    *   *Target Conversion:* 10% of active users.

### 1.2 Connectivity & Affiliates
*   **Global eSIM Hub:** 15-20% commission on every co-branded data plan.
    *   *Avg. Commission:* $4.00 per activation.
*   **Vibe Store Affiliates:** 15-20% commission on all synthesized gear and rentals.
    *   *Avg. Commission:* $35.00 per full kit deployment.
*   **Consolidated Bookings:** 3-8% commission on Flights, Hotels, and Cruises.
    *   *Avg. Commission:* $25.00 per hotel stay; $24.00 per flight.

### 1.3 Synthesis Credits
*   **Veo 3 Cinematic Teasers:** $5.00 per export (for non-Elite users).
*   **Imagen 4 Postcards:** $4.99 per printed/mailed physical card.
*   **Visual Path Finder:** $5.00 per aesthetic synthesis.

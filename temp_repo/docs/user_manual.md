# Backpacker: User Manual

## 1. The Intelligence Hub
Use the **Vision Hub** to decode the world. 
- **Chronos Lens**: Point at a building to see its 100-year-old self.
- **Pharmacy Scout**: Understand foreign medication ingredients.
- **Menu Explorer**: Translate and get culinary context for local dishes.

## 2. Safety & SOS
In high-stress situations, visit the **SOS Hub**. 
- **Panic Button**: Hold for 3 seconds. This will log your GPS, notify contacts, and can emit a loud siren to deter threats.
- **Safety Assistant**: Access local emergency numbers and essential phrases instantly.

## 3. Financial Optimization
Monitor your **Smart Wallet** through the **Budget Synthesis** page. The Financial Oracle will tell you if you're over-budget and suggest "Smart Swaps" in your itinerary to save money while keeping the trip's vibe.

## 4. Viral Sharing
Generate an **AI Trip Odyssey** teaser for your social media. Choose the "Social (9:16)" format for a TikTok-ready 1080p cinematic video of your journey highlights.
